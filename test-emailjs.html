Setup Instructions

Step 1: EmailJS Account Setup

  1. Go to EmailJS.com
  2. Sign up for a free account
  3. Verify your email address

Step 2: Email Service Setup

  1. In EmailJS dashboard, go to "Email Services"
  2. Click "Add New Service"
  3. Choose "Gmail"
  4. Connect your Gmail account (arcvantagedesignstudios@gmail.com)
  5. Note down your Service ID (e.g., service_abc123)

Step 3: Email Template Setup

  1. Go to "Email Templates"
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Use the template from the setup guide
  4. Note down your Template ID (e.g., template_xyz789)

Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • Emails not received: Check spam/junk folder
  • JavaScript errors: Open browser console (F12) and check for errors
  • Wrong credentials: Double-check your Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key
  • Gmail connection: Make sure your Gmail account is properly connected in EmailJS
